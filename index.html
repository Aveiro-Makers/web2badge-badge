web2badge-Badge

Web2badge is the first project of Aveiro Makers. That will be presented at Mini Maker Faire Lisbon on September 19, 2014. Web2badge is a system composed of a physycal badge with an LCD, a gateway that conects a group of badges to the internet and a website that comunicates with the gateway.

Content

web2badge-Badge repository have all electronic; firmware and mechanical info of the physical Badge. There are other repositorys with other parts of the system: web2badge-website; web2badge-gateway.
